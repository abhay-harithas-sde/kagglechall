Competition Rules
Please read carefully before submitting.

Eligibility
This competition is open to NxtWave Masterclass students only
One account per participant - no team submissions
You must join this competition before the deadline to submit
What You Must Submit
Every submission must include all 3 deliverables inside your submission.csv:

1. submission.csv (upload here on Kaggle)
Must contain exactly 15 answers - one per question (Q01-Q15)
Questions and answers are auto-encoded by the starter notebook
Do not manually edit the CSV - let the notebook generate it
Missing rows = score deduction
2. Streamlit App URL (entered in the notebook before generating CSV)
Deploy your chatbot to Streamlit Community Cloud (free)
The URL must be publicly accessible and working
App must be able to answer HR questions live
Entered in the last cell of the notebook - automatically saved into the CSV
3. LangSmith Trace URL (entered in the notebook before generating CSV)
Enable LangSmith tracing in your RAG pipeline
Share a public project link from your LangSmith dashboard
Traces must show your full RAG chain execution
Entered in the last cell of the notebook - automatically saved into the CSV
⚠️ Both links are validated automatically - missing or invalid links = score 0

Technical Rules
You must use RAG - direct LLM answers without retrieval will score low
Answers must be based only on the provided HR policy documents
Do not hardcode answers - your pipeline must actually retrieve and generate
Any LLM is allowed (Groq, Gemini, OpenAI, etc.)
Any embedding model is allowed
LangChain is recommended but not mandatory
Academic Integrity
Sharing your submission.csv with other students is not allowed
Plagiarism detection will be run on all submissions
Submissions found to be copied will be disqualified
You may discuss approaches, but code and outputs must be your own
Submission Limit
Maximum 5 submissions per day
Your best score will appear on the leaderboard
Questions?
Post in the Discussion tab or reach out to your instructor.

Good luck! 🚀
